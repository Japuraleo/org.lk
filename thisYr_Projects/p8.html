Project Name: Notifier


The ultimate goal of the "Notifier" project is to improve the literacy and creative expression of our club members. By fostering artistic expression, the project aims to transcend societal barriers, deepen understanding among individuals, and create a stronger sense of community. The platform encourages members to express their thoughts and emotions creatively, enabling them to connect on a deeper level, not only within the club but also with the broader society.
