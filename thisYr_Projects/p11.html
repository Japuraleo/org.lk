Project Name: Essence


ncouraging creativity may help us understand and sympathize with others, especially in a divided society. Creativity serves as a guiding light, pulling us away from violence and toward kindness and compassion, ultimately replacing hatred with love. Despite our many feelings, such as sadness, anguish, pleasure, or happiness, we are unified. Sharing viewpoints through writing, poetry, and art is an effective approach to increasing awareness and appreciation for our environment. Many people nowadays desire to be writers since it allows them to share their expertise openly and professionally, even with strangers. The ultimate goal of the Archoden initiative is to increase the literacy of our club members. We hope that through artistic expression, we may bridge gaps, increase understanding, and foster a sense of community.
