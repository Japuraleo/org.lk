Project Name: Apekshawe Handawa


In collaboration with the Volunteer Sri Lanka team, we initiated a heartfelt project at Apeksha Hospital, Maharagama. Through 'Apekshawe Handawa' we dedicated our efforts to cleaning ward no 18 and providing essential items for the patients. This project highlights our commitment to making a difference fostering teamwork, compassion, and community spirit, while reinforcing the values of leadership empathy, and service. Together, we strive to bring hope and care to those in need.
