Project Name: Archoden


Encouraging creativity may help us understand and sympathize with others, especially in a divided society. Creativity serves as a guiding light, pulling us away from violence and toward kindness and compassion, ultimately replacing hatred with love. Despite our many feelings, such as sadness, anguish, pleasure, or happiness, we are unified. Sharing viewpoints through writing, poetry, and art is an effective approach to increasing awareness and appreciation for our environment. Many people nowadays desire to be writers since it allows them to share their expertise openly and professionally, even with strangers. The ultimate goal of the Archoden initiative is to increase the literacy of our club members. We hope that through artistic expression, we may bridge gaps, increase understanding, and foster a sense of community.


In July, Malala Day (12th of July), World Youth Skills Day (15th of July), International Nelson Mandela Day (18th of July), and International Friendship Day (30th of July) were celebrated through sharing flyers on several social media platforms.
