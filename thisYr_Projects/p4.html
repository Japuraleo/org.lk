Project Name: Leo Tree Plantation Challenge 2024


Jpura Leos participated in the International Leo Tree Plantation Challenge on August 24th, with members planting trees in their home gardens. This initiative showcased our dedication to environmental sustainability and community action, symbolizing growth and hope. By planting trees like Mango, Karanda, and Money Plant, we joined a global effort to fight climate change, enhance air quality, and boost biodiversity. This event not only rejuvenates our local ecosystem but also contributes to a worldwide reforestation movement benefiting future generations
