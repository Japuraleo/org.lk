Project Name: Lead With Leoism 5.0


The orientation for newly appointed Club Officers was completed with 39 participants diving into Leoism from early morning till afternoon. The program highlighted Leoism's societal impact, project planning, and personal growth. A standout moment was the vibrant photoshoot showcasing the officers unity and dedication. This event set a solid base for new Club Officers, boosting their confidence and commitment to the Leo movement
