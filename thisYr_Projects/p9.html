Project Name: Keep Me - Trash Free


The "Keep me - Trash free" project conducted by the Leo Club of District 306 C2, University of Sri Jayewardenepura, on 28th September 2024 at Mount Lavinia Beach, is a comprehensive environmental and social service initiative focused on preserving the cleanliness and beauty of one of Sri Lanka's most popular beach destinations. The Club's leadership team engaged with Linea Aqua Pvt Ltd through phone conversations and emails to finalize the project's details. Linea Aqua Pvt Ltd eventually agreed to collaborate, offering their support during the cleanup event.
