Project Name: Chai Stall


On 23rd September, our Leo Club of District 306 D7 University of Sri Jayewardenepura organized a chai stall as part of our fundraising efforts.

Our enthusiastic members and committed project coordinators worked together to make the stall look stunning and run efficiently. The event garnered a lot of interest and helped us raise a respectable amount of money, which will go directly toward our next events and worthwhile community service activities.

It was more than just a fundraising event; it was a time of unity, ingenuity, and collaboration that demonstrated the Leoism spirit and the dedication we uphold as a community.
