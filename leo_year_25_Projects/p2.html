Project Name: Mehendi & Ice Cream Stall


On 25th July, our Leo Club of District 306 D7 University of Sri Jayewardenepura organized a vibrant Mehendi stall as part of our fundraising efforts. With the collective support of our energetic members and dedicated project coordinators, the stall was both beautifully presented and smoothly managed. The event attracted great attention and allowed us to raise a reasonable amount of funds, which will directly contribute to our upcoming events and meaningful community service projects. More than just a fundraiser, it was a moment of unity, creativity, and teamwork showcasing the spirit of Leoism and the commitment we carry forward together.
